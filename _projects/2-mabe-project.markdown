MABE (<a href="https://github.com/Hintzelab/MABE/wiki">Modular Agent Based Evolver</a>) is an evolutionary computation framework developed by the Hintze Lab. The software allows for highly flexible and replicable digital evolution experiments.

In its current form, MABE is most useful for seasoned evolutionary computation researchers. As we move forward with MABE development, the goal is to make it more user-friendly not just for biologists without coding experience, but even for undergraduate or high school student users. 

I am working with the rest of the MABE development team to help design a prepackaged version of MABE that is user-friendly yet powerful for use in educational settings.
